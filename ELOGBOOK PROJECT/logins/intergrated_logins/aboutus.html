Our background

Namasuba college of commerce was established in 1995. it was later registered by both
the Registrar of Companies as Namasuba College of Commerce and Ministry of Education
and Sports in Uganda with a licence by National Council for Hihger Education to train
Business, Technical and Vocational Courses.

The college is externally assessed by the Uganda Business and Technical Examinations
Board (UBTEB), Directorate of Industrial Training (DIT), Makerere University and Avance
International University for ECD, Uganda Business Technical Vocational Training Institution
(BTVET) and inspected by the Directorate of Education Standard Agency (DESA).

Namasuba College of Commerce is a large autonomous instititution located on Entebe
Road at Namasuba Town, 4kms from Kampala.

Namasuba College of Commerce is the major provider of study text books, examinations
and manuals for the students and proffessionals in Africa and else where. these include;
kuguka mu Busubuzi, Great ideas for visionary success and many more.

We conduct Radio and TV shows on various media stations in which we sammarise relevant
materials contained in text books and present this in a concise form so as to help students
enjoy a clear Undesrtanding of the topics presented.

The 24 years of eperience and constant growth makes us a one-time stop centre for
development studies.
